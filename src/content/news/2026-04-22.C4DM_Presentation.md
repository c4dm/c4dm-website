### C4DM Seminar:  AIM / C4DM research presentation to students, a PhD research overview
-----------------

#### QMUL, School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science

#### Centre for Digital Music Seminar Series

**Seminar by:** C4DM PhD Students

**Date/time:**  Wednesday, 22nd April 2026, 11:30am

**Location:** G2, Engineering Building, Mile End Campus

<b>Title</b>: AIM / C4DM research presentation to students, a PhD research overview
-----------------

<b>Abstract</b>:  
Four PhD student from AIM CDT, based at C4DM, will do a short presentation about their latest research and/or some of the research done during their PhD. This is a presentation focus on undergraduate and master students. A presentation and outreach activity to encourage students to think about a research career option. 
The four students have different research focus to give the broad overview of AIM and C4DM research

<b>Bio</b>:  
Jackson James Loth:
Time to vibe together: cloud-based guitar and intelligent agent

Minhui Lu
Differentiable Physical Modelling for Singing Voice Synthesis

Ashley Laurent Noel-Hirst
Latent Spaces for Human-AI music generation

Adam Andrew Garrow
A computational model of music cognition using statistical learning of structures
